How we calculate this?

Balance Calculation

Your Available Balance is calculated by taking your current ledger balance and subtracting any pending outgoing transactions (like card purchases or transfers) while adding any pending incoming transactions (like deposits).

The Pending amount reflects transactions that have been authorized but not yet posted to your ledger. Once they post, they will adjust your ledger balance accordingly.
